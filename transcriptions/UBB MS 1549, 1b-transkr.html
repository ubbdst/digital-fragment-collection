First Menota draft, 2004-06

The text is from vespers and matins of The Annunciation (25 March). The fragment was a gift to the Bergen museum by cand. theol. H. Daae in 1829. Missing letters or words in hymns, antiphons, responsories and prayers have been supplied from Breviarium Monasticum, summorum pontificum cura recognitum, Marietti, Taurini et Romae 1963. Missing letters or words in the readings (Bede"s homily for the annunciation) are supplied from the online version of Patrologia latina, http://pld.chadwyck.co.uk/
