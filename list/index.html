Digital fragment collection

This website presents electronic images of the fragments together with catalogue text for each one, and transcriptions and translations of selected fragments.

This electronic collection includes fragments in the University of Bergen library, and the Bergen State Archives.

For more manuscript fragments, see Inventory of medieval manuscript fragments in Norway.
