Description of State Archive of Bergen, MS B 43, 3-4

The Missal at State Archive of Bergen

Introduction

The four fragments in the State Archive of Bergen are from two different manuscripts, one Antiphonary (with songs for the Hours) and one Missal (with the songs, prayers and readings for the Mass).
